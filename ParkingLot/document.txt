Functional Requirements:
1.The parking lot can have multiple levels.

2.Each level has multiple parking spots.

3.There are three types of vehicles: Motorcycle, Car, and Truck.

4.Each vehicle can only be parked in spots that fit it:

5.Motorcycle can park in motorcycle, compact, or large spots.

6.Car can park in compact or large spots.

7.Truck can only park in large spots.

The system should support the following operations:

8.parkVehicle(vehicle) — parks the vehicle in an available spot.

9.leaveSpot(vehicle) — frees up the spot occupied by the vehicle.

10.getAvailableSpots(level, spotType) — returns number of available spots of a given type on a level.
